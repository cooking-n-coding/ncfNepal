Family Ministry

Strengthening the foundation of homes and hearts with biblical wisdom...

Date: March 2025

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) has been working in the field of family life for the past 18 years primarily through training. LTD provided TOT (training of trainers) to 59 couples, and two three-day Basic Family training to 158 couples through residential and online modes and premarital counseling seminars to 43 teenagers and adults from different churches in two different regions in the past six months. 16 couples and 8 singles completed two days premarital counseling TOT held from 28-29 Aug 2024.

Date: February 2024

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) has been working in the field of family life for the past 17 years primarily through training. LTD provided TOT (training of trainers) to 31 couples and a three-day Family refresher training for 26 couples in Pokhara in the past 6 months. The participants’ report from the refresher training states that they have trained 1200 couples with 1-2 days of training and provided counselling to 100 couples in the past 3 years. LTD also provided premarital counselling seminars to 450 teenagers from different churches in two different regions.

Abinash and Laxmi
Member - Family Ministry

Abinash and Laxmi are from the hilly region of Western Rukum, a district with just 0.9% Christians in the total population. Abinash is a pastor, and his wife Laxmi is an Elder and women's leader in the Musikot Assembly of God (AOG) church. They have completed 3 different levels of Family Ministry since 2017. They have been facilitating and organizing basic family training once or twice a year in Western and Eastern Rukum in collaboration with local churches. They have trained more than 1000 couples in the past 7 years. They are motivated by several reasons to facilitate and organize the training. Firstly, it is a new teaching to the church. They have felt the need for this kind of education in the church. Secondly, the impact it has had on participants’ family life. It has reduced the conflict between husband and wife, with forgiveness and reconciliation in couples who have filed for divorce. It has also improved their own marriage and family life. They faced some challenges in facilitating and organizing basic family training. Some pastors/leaders are not participating in the training. They think that they already know about family life and have a lot of experience. “I would like to thank LTD for allowing me to participate in the Family Ministry training. It has strengthened our marriage and enabled us to equip others in rural and remote areas of Nepal” shares Abinash.
