Church Leadership Development

This ministry area of LTD aims to enable existing and potential church leaders to develop their knowledge, skills, character and confidence needed for their leadership roles in the church. It inspires church leaders to embrace a continuous learning attitude, ensuring they remain adaptable and effective in their roles. Additionally, LTD emphasizes the importance of pastoral care and mentoring, supporting leaders and members alike in their spiritual journeys. Comprehensive church leadership training includes residential, online and modular formats and short-term leadership training courses.

Women Leadership Development

The ministry area emphasizes the vital role of women in church ministry, promoting their leadership and engagement in various aspects of Church and community life. It focuses on life and ministry skills, character, and knowledge to empower women, equipping them with the tools for effective leadership and personal growth. A range of leadership seminars and training programs are conducted, including residential, modular, and virtual formats.

Family Relationship

LTD believes that the healthy family is the foundation of a healthy community. This area of ministry provides the knowledge and skills necessary for building and maintaining relationships, ensuring everyone can contribute to a harmonious and supportive environment. By promoting Christian culture and values within individuals, families, the Church, and the broader community, the plan seeks to instill a sense of unity and shared purpose. LTD will organize various family-oriented programs, including basic family training, Training of Trainers (ToT), refresher courses, pre-marital counseling seminars and pre-marital counselling training.
