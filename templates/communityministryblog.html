Community Ministry

Building Stronger Communities, One Story at a Time through biblical guidance...

Date: July 2022

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) We organized an awareness program on Child Rights for 230 secondary school students from 4 different schools in Baireni, Dhading. This program helped them to know about child rights. LTD trained 50 women in basic book keeping in the Women Self Help Group. This training equipped them to keep their financial transactions in a proper way.

Ranju Pariyar
Community development and relief work

Ranju Pariyar is from a poor Dalit family. Her family didn't support her study. Her father is an alcoholic and always quarrels with the family. LTD supports her study. She said "I was going to drop out of school because of my family problems and was unable to purchase the school uniform and educational materials. One of my brothers near my house, Ram Kalikote, who is Christian, came to me and I shared with him about the struggle in continuing my study. Then, LTD started supporting me by providing me with educational materials since grade 9. This support has helped me to continue my study. I would like to express my thanks to NCFN " LTD responded to the needs of poor and marginalized people when their lives were affected by a massive fire Bardiya. We distributed relief materials including mattresses and blankets to 40 families. They were daily laborers, widows, and elderly people. We also distributed school uniforms and educational materials to 50 children who were from poor and Dalit families. All the relief items were distributed in coordination with the local government. The village chairman and principals of the school expressed their gratitude to the organization for helping the vulnerable people and appreciated LTD's work.

Date: January 2021

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) responded to the need of poor people when their lives were badly hit by COVID-19. The relief materials included food items, blankets, jackets, tents, and nets. About 900 families from 10 different parts of Nepal were the beneficiaries of this help. They were daily wages workers, prisoners, physically challenged, and old aged people. All these items were distributed in coordination with the local government. All the village chairmen expressed their gratitude to the organization for helping the vulnerable people and appreciated our work which was supposed to be the responsibility of the government.

Kalchu Saud
Community development and relief work

Kalchu Saud migrated from the remote hills of far west (Acham) to the plain (Kanchanpur) of far west Nepal 12 years ago due to the Maoist insurgency. She lives in ailani land (government’s land) along with 20 other families near the national park. Her son and daughter-in-law are migrant workers in India and she is left with their son. LTD/NCF Nepal provided fiber blankets to all the households of this village the last Nov. In Nepal, old people die during the night in the winter as they don’t have warm clothes to cover. After receiving the blanket there was a big smile on Kalchu’s face and she expressed her joy saying “until now I used to spend the whole night awake in the cold season, now I can have sound sleep.”
