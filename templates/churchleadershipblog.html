Church Leadership

Future leaders trained with boldness and biblical values...

Date: March 2025

The Leadership Training Department's (LTD) mission is to empower the local churches through leadership development. So, LTD offers church leadership training through online, offline (residential), and modular modes. We have trained 63 pastors and lay leaders from different local churches through online, offline, and modular modes in the past one year. The offline (residential) training is for two months and runs in Butwal, 200 km west of Kathmandu. LTD organized a 3-day Church Leadership Training Alumni Conference in Butwal from 5-7 May 2024. 110 pastors and leaders joined the conference. The theme of the conference was from Philippians 1:5. The features of the conference were to celebrate God’s work, refresh the spirit, and be ready for a hard time. Rev P. Charles Jo encouraged from the word of God. Church Leadership Trainings in modular mode is going on in 2 different small towns of west Nepal with 55 church pastors and leaders. LTD organized a 4-day Community Bible Study (CBS) training for 33 church leaders mostly women leaders in Dec 2024. This training was conducted in partnership with Community Bible Study (CBS) and the local churches. It has helped the trainees to conduct bible studies for those who come from an oral culture.

Amar Singh
Member - Church Leadership Training

I am Amar Singh Subarnakar from Nepalgung-12, Banke, a Jyoti Niwas AG church member. I completed 3 months of Church Leadership Training from LTD/NCFN online. I didn’t expect too much from this online training. But this is something that changed my thinking, attitude, and behavior. The subjects delivered are very important and relevant for leadership development. All teachers are well educated, experienced, and skillful. This training helped me to grow in Christ through spiritual discipline. I daily spend 20 minutes reading the bible and praying with God. Now, my lifestyle is different than before. Previously, I read the bible but didn’t understand. The bible study method helped me to use different techniques and skills to understand the word of God.

Date: February 2023

Modular Course, Rajapur, Bardiya, Nepal
The Leadership Training Department's (LTD), in collaboration with a local Christian body, conducted its two Modular Trainings in Rajapur, Bardiya, far west of Nepal bordering India, in November, 2022 and February, 2023. Our trainees came from Tharu and Sonaha ethnic communities. The trainings on biblical counselling and biblical preaching were a huge success, participants stated. They are all engaged in some sort of ministry capacity in their respective churches. This form of training is a new strategic venture of LTD for capacity building of the local churches. The participants of the first two trainings expressed that they acquired valuable confidence, capacity, and skills for counseling and preaching.

Ramsuraj
Participant - Church Leadership Training

Ramsuraj from Uttar Pradesh, India, bordering Nepal, came to Christ after his mother’s healing. After joining Assemblies of God church in southern Nepal, he was sent to 3 months of Church Leadership Training in Butwal in Feb, 2022. Ramsuraj states, “Different teachers and pastors taught different topics which helped me to know more about God. I understood the importance of God's word. I was an angry man, I said bad words to others. But this training helped me to know how to love others and forgive each other. I learned and got skills on how to study the bible and preach God's word.” Now, Ramsuraj is one of the leaders of a local church with 50-55 believers. He also went to a new place to preach the gospel and has started a small church and is committed to planting more churches. LTD offers 3 months of church leadership training through online and offline modes. We trained 27 church leaders through online mode and 12 through offline mode in the Far East Nepal training station in the second half of 2022.
