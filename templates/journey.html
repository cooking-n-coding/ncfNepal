A strategic move in LTD ministry

Manoj Pradhananga (Director of LTD/NCFN Nepal)

Date: March 2025

LTD is soon going to complete 20 years of ministry. It is an indigenous church leadership training ministry to equip leaders women, pastors, lay leaders and families. We have changed our strategy according to the context. Initially, all our trainings were residential. During Covid, we moved to online mode and now we are moving to modular mode.
The training impact evaluation clearly indicates the need to move to modular mode of training where the pastors and lay leaders from local churches come together and learn to develop the local churches both in quality and quantity. Thus, LTD is now focusing on the local church community as a training hub. By and large there is a strong church community that comprise different church groups and denominations in every region of Nepal.
Secondly, LTD ministry is involving more in community engage ment. How can LTD ministry impact the local community in part nership with the local churches where LTD has extended the ministry? This is the reason, now, that LTD is focusing its resources in a few local areas.
Thirdly, LTD is going to play a supporting role. LTD has served the Nepali church for nearly 2 decades. Now, we see Nepali churches are able to organize and financially support church leadership training. So, LTD will support the local churches in the areas of teaching only. The local churches have to organize and manage financially. We believe this is a genuine partnership with the local church and a real growth of the organization that helps it to be sustainable. Family Ministry Finally, we are grateful to God who continues to lead us even in the time of change and grateful to each partner who continues to support us to fulfill His call through the ministry of LTD/NCF Nepal.
