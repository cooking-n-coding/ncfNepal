Women Ministry

Through this ministry, countless women have found strength and guidance...

Date: March 2025

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) organized women’s leadership training on residential, online and modular mode and trained 71 leaders in the past one year. The residential training was organized in Lalitpur, Kathmandu and Butwal, the west while modular was organized in a small town of west Nepal called Rajapur. 5 women's leadership seminars were organized in the remote villages of Makwanpur, Sunsari, Baglung, Dhading, and Lamjung where 537 women participated in these 2-day seminars in the last year. Also, former trainees were visited in the training areas during the seminars. LTD organized a 3-day Women’s Leadership Training Alumni Conference in Aradhana Church, Lalitpur from February 16-18, 2025. 135 women leaders joined the conference. The theme of the conference was 2 Corinthians 1:5.

Rina Shahi
Member - Women's Leadership Training

I am Rina Shahi Thakuri from Ratomate 15, Hetauda, one of the women leaders of Pabitra Church. I completed a two month women's leadership training through an online mode run by LTD/NCFN. I came to know about this training through my pastor’s wife who has already completed the training. I was looking for training so that I can learn the word of God and grow in Christian life. I couldn’t go to long-term residential training because of the children and household work. This online training was suitable for me. This training helped me to grow in Christ and learn many topics. The topics were very important and especially the biblical preaching was good for me. Before this training, I used to preach from the bible just reading the word and explaining what I know but after the training it helped me to prepare and deliver the sermon. I thank you LTD/NCFN for allowing me to join the training.

Date: February 2023

The Leadership Training Department (LTD) hosted a South Asia Women Leadership Summit 2022 in Kathmandu from 5-7 Nov 2022. 21 women participated from Nepal, Bangladesh, and India. Three women from the USA facilitated the training on forming the ministry vision. The training contained group work, role play, Bible study, life map work, and presenting the ministry vision. It helped to make good relationships within the Nepali, Indian, and Bangladeshi women leaders and find a calling from God in their own lives. The training on leadership development was held on 9-10 Nov 2022 preaching methods stood out to me. Being a minister in the church, I have to preach from the Bible and teach other believers. But I did not know the methods and skills. This training is a blessing for me personally, my family, and my church. I will pray and support LTD by sending other women in training and promoting the training.” LTD organized two residential women's leadership trainings in Kathmandu and Butwal (west). The same program was organized in Kathmandu. 39 women who had completed Women Leadership Training (WLT) participated in the training. Most of them were pastors' wives and all of them were having some kind of leadership roles in the church. They were trained in decision-making, conflict resolution, spending time with the Lord in silence, and leadership qualities. This training helped women to build their mentoring skills, increase their leadership qualities, and learn from each other’s sharing.
